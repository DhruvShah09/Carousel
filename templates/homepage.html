About Us

What is Carousel? Well, think back to your childhood and remember that classic carnival attraction. That carousel introduced you to new things as you rode in a circle, and our Carousel does the same thing.

This software, rooted in the academic life at Georgia Tech, allows you to find new people to study with. "Riding the Carousel" will let you to create new or join already existing study groups for any of your classes.

To ride, first tell us all the classes you're currently taking, and then you'll be able to see what groups are at your disposal to maximize your academic success.
